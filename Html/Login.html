Welcome to Pixelforge Academy

Pixelforge Academy is a learning platform designed for students and beginners who want to understand how video games are created. By logging in, you gain access to beginner friendly explanations, tools, and resources that explore the game development process from idea to finished game. Whether you are new to game development or simply curious about how games are made, Pixelforge Academy provides a structured and approachable learning experience.

What This Form Is For

This registration form allows you to create an account and access Pixelforge Academy's learning content. The information you provide helps personalise your experience and recommend game development topics that match your interests.

How to Complete the Form

Begin by entering your basic account details, including a valid email address, username, and password. You will then be asked to select your preferred game engine and indicate your current coding skill level to help tailor learning materials to your experience.

Learning Preferences & Submission

Finally, choose how you prefer to learn, describe your game development goals, and agree to the Academy's terms of service. Once completed, submit the form to continue and begin exploring beginner friendly game development resources.
